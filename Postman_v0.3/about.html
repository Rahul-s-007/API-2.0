About Us

CrimeLog is a digital map displaying the location of crimes committed over the last month, along with its nature , so you can stay informed and take appropriate precautions. Whether you are moving to a new place, just a tourist or like us curious, CrimeLog is the tool for you. Simply open the website, input the city for which you wish to read crime data, and then hit enter. Voila! you'll be sent to a new page with a heat map of the crimes and with information on types of crimes committed in your area.It's that easy!
